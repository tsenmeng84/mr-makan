Steakhouse – Rating: 9.0/10

Reviewed by: tsenmeng

Juicy and flavorful Australian grain-fed steak, cooked to perfection, shines at this cozy, simple eatery. Generous sides complement the meal, all at reasonable prices. While they don't serve alcohol, the halal offerings make it a delightful choice for diners, ensuring a satisfying experience that's hard to beat.

Fish 'n' Chips – Rating: 9.0/10

Reviewed by: tsenmeng

The fish and chips impress with a light, crispy batter encasing fresh fish. While the modern ambiance suits families, the venue can become quite crowded, especially on weekends, and reservations are not accepted. Overall, it's a reasonable choice for a delightful meal, though be prepared for potential wait times.

Madam Kwan's | Truly Malaysian Cuisine

Malay – Rating: 9.0/10

Reviewed by: tsenmeng

The nasi lemak beef rendang impresses with its tender beef and flavorful sambal, creating a delightful experience. Although the service lags and the prices are steep, the cozy ambiance makes it ideal for family gatherings. A satisfying dish, though the pace may test your patience.
